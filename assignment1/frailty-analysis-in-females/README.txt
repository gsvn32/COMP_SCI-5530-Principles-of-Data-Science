Frailty is physical weakness; lack of health or strength. Reduced grip strength in females correlated with higher frailty scores and vice versa. 
Hand grip strength can be quantified by measuring the amount of static force that the hand can squeeze around a dynamometer. 
The data is collected from 10 female participants. 
- The Height is measured in inches
- Weight in pounds
- Age in years
- Grip strength in kilograms
